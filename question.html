Frequently Asked Questions



Q. How can I pay for ingredients?

A. You can use your money directly(e.g. credit card, bank transfer) or use points of YUMCIPE.

Q. How can I get points?

A. When you share your recipes or get a LIKE from many users in YUMCIPE, we’ll give you points.

Q. Ingredients that I bought in YUMCIPE have gone bad. I want to get a refund.

A. We are a mediation platform so we just follow refund policies of individual foodstuff companies. If you meet problem in ingredients quality, we will pass on your complain to food suppliers and solve that problem ASAP.

Q. We are a foodstuff company, and we want to place our products for YUMCIPE users.

A. Thanks for your suggestion about a partnership. Please contact us or call to +1)206-123-4567 and then YUMCIPE can make a business meeting for making the partnership.

Q. How can I be selected as a best recipe provider?

A. The most important criterion for being chosen as a best recipe provider is the number of LIKE. The recipe consumer can click only one LIKE per account, and the recipe with the most LIKE in a week will be placed in the Hall of Fame. Furthermore, additional points benefits are offered to recipe providers listed in Hall of Fame.
